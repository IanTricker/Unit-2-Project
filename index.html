Save the Sea Otters

What are sea otters?

Sea otters are more than there cute looks, they are aquatic environmentalist. Sea otters eat sea urchins to help kelp forst flourish, and eat crabs to grow eelgrass. Sea otters have a size of 4 feet and weigh up to 70 pounds.

More on sea otters
Sea otters are going extinct

Sea otters are on the endangered species list acording to International Union for the Conservation of Nature and Natural Resources (IUCN).
what is causing it:

  • Predation and Natural Threats
  • Environmental Pollution
  • Disease
  • Climate Change
  • Human Interference

How can we prevent it?

The best way to protect the sea otters is:

  • Legislative Measures
  • Pollution Control
  • Restoration Projects
  • Rescue and Rehabilitation Initiatives
  • Scientific Research and Monitoring
  • Public Education and Community Involvement
